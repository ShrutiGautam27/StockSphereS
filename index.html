      FAQ

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Is my money actually at risk on StockSphereS?+
      No. All trading on StockSphereS is done with virtual money in a simulated environment. You cannot lose real money. The platform is designed purely for education and skill development before you ever touch a real brokerage account.
      How strict is the AI Mentor?+
      Very. The AI is designed to not sugarcoat your mistakes. If you make a foolish trade out of FOMO, it will call it trash. The goal is to break bad emotional habits and force you into a disciplined, data-driven mindset.
      What is the Discipline Score?+
      The Discipline Score is a quantitative rating (0–100) of how well you stick to your trading plan. It measures things like whether you cut losses at your predefined stop-loss, avoid emotional trades during volatility, and execute strategies consistently over time.
